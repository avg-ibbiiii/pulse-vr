ABOUT PULSE VR

Manchester's premier virtual reality gaming centre

THE STORY

Pulse VR opened in Manchester college City campus with one goal: making high-quality virtual reality accessible to everyone not just those who can afford the hardware at home.

Starting with 8 headsets and a single room, the centre has grown into 3 dedicated gaming suites with over 50 VR experiences and more than 10,000 visitors through the doors (This is all a lie btw) .

Solo adventurers, school groups, corporate team-building, birthday parties Pulse VR is built for everyone. The accessibility programme ensures visitors with disabilities get the exact same world-class experience as everyone else.

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2026

ℹ️ This is a demonstration website built for a BTEC Level 3 Computing assignment. No real personal data is collected.

1. Who We Are

Pulse VR is a virtual reality gaming centre in Manchester. For UK GDPR purposes, we are the data controller for any personal data you provide when using this site or booking a session.

2. What We Collect

When making a booking: your name, email address, phone number (optional), booking preferences, and any accessibility requirements you choose to share. We do not collect payment information online.

3. How We Use It

To send a booking confirmation, contact you if rescheduling is needed, and provide any accessibility accommodations requested. We will never sell your data or use it for marketing without your explicit consent.

4. Legal Basis

Contract performance under UK GDPR you give us your details to make a booking and we need them to fulfil it.

5. Retention

Booking records are kept for 12 months then securely deleted. Email privacy@pulsevr.co.uk to request earlier deletion.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you can access, correct, delete or restrict your data. You can also complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

7. Cookies

This site uses no tracking cookies. The only browser storage is sessionStorage for your text size preference — cleared when you close the tab.

8. Contact

Privacy queries: privacy@pulsevr.co.uk or 42 Stevenson Square, Manchester, M1 1FB.
